About

The intention of color grep is to help you picking an exact color from almost any image.

Why this and not a browser extension?

Extensions are good to pick color from images already on the browser, not that good for local images. And anyway, I prefer to do a complete webapp :)

What's been used to do it?

HTML(5), CSS(3), pure JavaScript and colour.js (for color calculations)

Drag & Drop HTML5 JavaScript API, Canvas and hamsters.
